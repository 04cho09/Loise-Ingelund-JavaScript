Rating and why

I would personally not give this movie a 5-star rating as the movie simply does not have a typical structure, whilst it's not bad it was just hard for me to follow it. But the movie is supposed to be confusing as Bateman could be perceived as an unreliable narrator, but it is just was not my style. Since I still give it a 4,5-star rating, means that I did enjoy it greatly, I'm just not a fan of it's "open ending" meaning movies where the ending isn't much of an ending since it's up to the person watching to imagin what happens next with the set up the movie gives you. But as I've mentioned, I am a big fan of gore and more specifically blood, which this movie features a lot.
